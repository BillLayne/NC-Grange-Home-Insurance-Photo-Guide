Getting Started

A Quick Photo Guide for Your Home Insurance

Welcome! To complete your NC Grange Mutual application, we just need a few photos of your property. This is a standard requirement for homes built more than 30 years ago, and this secure guide will walk you through exactly what to photograph.

Don't worry - this is easy! It only takes about 10 minutes.

How it works: Use your phone's camera to take the pictures for each step. All photos will be saved to your phone's photo gallery. At the end, we'll show you how to send them to us.

Step 1 of 6: The Outside of Your Home

What we need:

Walk around your house and take 4 photos, one of each side:

  • The Front
  • The Back
  • Left side & Right side

Tip: Stand back far enough to get the whole side of the house in the photo, from the ground to the roof.

❓ Having trouble getting the shot?

If trees or other objects are in the way, that's okay! Just do your best to capture as much of the house as you can.

Step 2 of 6: Outbuildings and Structures

What we need:

Take one photo of any other structures on your property, like:

  • Detached garages
  • Sheds or barns
  • Carports or workshops
❓ What if I don't have any other buildings?

No problem! Just click the "Next Step" button to continue. You can skip taking photos for this step.

Step 3 of 6: Main Living Room

What we need:

Take 1-2 photos of your main living or family room. We are not judging your housekeeping, just checking the general condition.

Tip: Stand in a corner of the room to get a wide shot that shows the floor, walls, and ceiling.

Step 4 of 6: The Kitchen

What we need:

Take 1-2 photos showing the overall condition of your kitchen. Try to show:

  • Cabinets
  • Countertops
  • Flooring

Tip: Turn on the lights to get a bright, clear picture.

Step 5 of 6: Under the Sinks (Plumbing)

What we need:

This is important to check for potential leaks. Please take a clear photo of the plumbing underneath:

  • The kitchen sink
  • All bathroom sinks

💡 Tip: The most important one! It is usually dark under the sink. Use your phone's flashlight to get a bright, clear photo of the pipes like the example.

❓ How do I turn on my phone's flashlight?

On iPhone: Swipe down from the top-right corner of your screen and tap the flashlight icon.
On Android: Swipe down from the top of your screen and look for the flashlight icon to tap.

Step 6 of 6: The Breaker Box (Electrical Panel)

What we need:

The final photo! Please find your electrical panel and open the metal door. Take one picture of the breakers inside.

Safety First: Do not touch anything inside the panel. Just open the door, take the photo, and close it.
❓ Where do I find the breaker box?

Common locations are the garage, basement, laundry room, or a utility closet.

You're All Done! Time to Submit.

Great job! You have gathered all the photos. Before sending, please do a final check to make sure you have everything.

Final Check:

  • ✅ The Front, Back, Left, and Right of your house (4 photos)
  • ✅ Any sheds or garages (or you skipped it)
  • ✅ The Living Room
  • ✅ The Kitchen
  • ✅ Under ALL sinks (kitchen + bathrooms)
  • ✅ The electrical panel (with the door OPEN)
